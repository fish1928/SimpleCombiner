1. SimpleCombiner.permutate is just a simple permutation function.
2. SimpleCombiner.combine is just a simple combination function.

usage:
  SimpleCombiner.permutate(['a','b','c'],3)
  SimpleCombiner.permutate(['a','b','c'],2)
  SimpleCombiner.permutate(['a','b','c'],4)

  SimpleCombiner.combine(['a','b','c'],3)
  SimpleCombiner.combine(['a','b','c'],2)
  SimpleCombiner.combine(['a','b','c'],4)
